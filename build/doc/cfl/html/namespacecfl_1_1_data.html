Constructs discount curve. The discount factor for maturity \(t\) is given by

\[ D(t) = \exp(-\Gamma(t) (t-t_0)), \]

where \(\Gamma =\) rYield (the yield curve) and \(t_0 = \) dInitialTime.

Constructs forward curve for a stock. The forward price at at time \(t\) is given by

\[ F(t) = S(t_0) \exp(-q(t-t_0))/D(t), \]

where \(S(t_0) = \) dSpot, \(q=\) dDividendYield, \(D(t) = \) rDiscount, and \(t_0 = \) dInitialTime.

Constructs stationary volatility curve. The value of volatility at time \(t\) is given by

\[ V(t) = \sigma \sqrt{\frac{\exp(2\lambda (t-t_0))-1}{2\lambda (t-t_0)}}, \]

where \(\sigma=\) dSigma, \(\lambda=\) dLambda, and \(t_0 = \) dInitialTime.

Parameters
dSigmaThe short-term volatility
dLambdaThe mean-reversion coefficient.
