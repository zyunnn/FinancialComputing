Computes forward curve

\[ F(t) = S_0 e^{q(t)(t-t_0)}, \quad t\in [t_0,t_M], \]

where cost-of-carry rate \(q=q(t)\) is obtained by the Steffen (monotone cubic spline) interpolation of the market cost-of-carry rates. On the first interval \([t_0,t_1]\), the cost-of-carry rate is constant:

\[ q(t) = q(t_1), \quad t\in [t_0,t_1]. \]

Parameters
dSpot( \(S_0\)) The spot price of the stock.
rDeliveryTimes( \((t_i)_{i=1,\dots,M}\)) The maturities of the market forward contracts, \(t_0<t_1\).
rForwardPrices( \((F(t_i))_{i=1,\dots,M}\)) The market forward prices.
dInitialTime( \(t_0\)) The initial time.
Returns
The forward curve obtained by the Steffen interpolation of the market cost-of-carry rates.

Computes implied volatility curve \(\Sigma = \Sigma(t)\) by applying interpolation method rInterp to variance curve

\[ V(t) = \Sigma^2(t)(t - t_0), \quad t\geq t_0. \]

The resulting volatility curve is constant on \([t_0,t_1]\).

Parameters
rTimes( \((t_i)_{i=1,\dots,M}\)) The maturities of the market implied volatilities, ( \(t_1>t_0\).
rVolsThe market implied volatilities.
dInitialTime( \(t_0\)) The initial time.
rInterpA monotone interpolation method.
Returns
cfl::Function The implied volatility curve obtained by the interpolation of market variances.
