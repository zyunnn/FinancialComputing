Computes cost-of-carry rate curve \(c=(c(t))_{t\geq t_0}\) for the Black model:

\[ q(t) = \theta \frac{1-e^{-\lambda(t-t_0)}}{\lambda(t-t_0)} + \frac{\sigma^2}2 \frac{1-e^{-2\lambda(t-t_0)}}{2\lambda(t-t_0)}, \quad t\geq t_0. \]

dLambda( \(\lambda\geq 0\)) The mean reversion level.
dSigma( \(\sigma\geq 0\)) The volatility.
dInitialTime( \(t_0\)) The initial time.
Returns
The cost-of-carry rate curve in Black model.

Computes the curve of forward prices ("clean" or "dirty") for a coupon bond. The bond pays coupons \(q\delta t\) at times \((t_i)_{i=1,\dots,M}\) such that

\[ t_0 < t_1 \leq t_0+\delta t, \quad t_{i+1}-t_i= \delta t, \quad t_M = T. \]

The bond also pays notional \(N=1\) at maturity \(T\). The buyer of the forward contract pays forward price \(F(t)\) at delivery time \(t\) and then receives coupons \(q\delta t\) at payments times \(t_i>t\) and notional \(N=1\) at maturity \(T\).

Parameters
dRate( \(q\)) The coupon rate.
dPeriod( \(\delta t\)) The time interval between payments.
dMaturity( \(T\)) The maturity.
rDiscountThe discount curve.
dInitialTime( \(t_0\)) The initial time.
bCleanThis parameter controls the output. If its value is true, then we compute "clean" prices. Otherwise, we compute "dirty" prices that include the accrued interest from the next coupon.
Returns
The forward price ("clean" or "dirty") curve for the coupon bond.

const cfl::Function &  rDiscount

Computes the curve of forward rates. It costs nothing to enter the forward rate agreement (FRA) with maturity \(t\) and period \(\delta t\) as either a borrower or a lender. Forward rate \(R^f(t,t+\delta t)\) is set at issue time \(t_0\). In the contract,

  1. At maturity \(t\), the borrower receives notional \(N\).
  2. At time \(t+\delta t\), the borrower pays the notional plus the interest computed at the forward rate:

    \[ N(1+ R^f(t,t+\delta t)\delta t). \]

Parameters
dPeriod( \(\delta t\)) The rate period.
rDiscountThe discount curve.
Returns
cfl::Function \((R^f(t,t+\delta t))_{t\geq t_0}\) The curve of forward rates.

Computes yield curve \(\gamma=\gamma(t)\) for the Svensson model:

\[ \gamma(t) = c_0 + c_1 \frac{1-e^{-\lambda_1(t-t_0)}}{\lambda_1 (t-t_0)} + c_2 \left(\frac{1-e^{-\lambda_1(t-t_0)}}{\lambda_1 (t-t_0)} - e^{-\lambda_1 (t-t_0)}\right) + c_3 \left(\frac{1-e^{-\lambda_2(t-t_0)}}{\lambda_2 (t-t_0)} - e^{-\lambda_2 (t-t_0)}\right) , \quad t\geq t_0. \]

Parameters
dC0( \(c_0\)) The first constant
dC1( \(c_1\)) The second constant.
dC2( \(c_2\)) The third constant.
dC3( \(c_3\)) The fourth constant.
dLambda1( \(\lambda_1>0\)) The first mean-reversion rate.
dLambda2( \(\lambda_2>0\)) The second mean-reversion rate, \(\lambda_1\not=\lambda_2\).
dInitialTime( \(t_0\)) The initial time.
Returns
The yield curve for the Svensson model.
